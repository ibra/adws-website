About Us

Welcome to the All Disable Welfare Society! We are a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of people with disabilities. Our mission is to promote inclusivity, provide support, and advocate for the rights of individuals with disabilities in our community.All Disable Welfare Society Balochistan was established in 2006 in District Kohlu, Balochistan Pakistan. The aim of establishing this society was to help the people with special needs.

The driving force behind ADWS is Mr Sanaullah Marri. Mr Sanullah Marri is a teacher by profession. He is blind by birth and has taken the mission of helping others to next level.

Aims and Objectives

  • To Help people with disbaility in getting quality education and providing them with self employment.
  • To provide Disable people with Wheel chairs, Mobility Canes, and other equipment that make them less dependent on others.
  • To collaborate and help other relief organization reach to far flung areas of Balochistan.
